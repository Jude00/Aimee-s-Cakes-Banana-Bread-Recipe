Ingredient List and Measurements

  • 3 medium sizes of very ripe Banana
  • 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter (melted)
  • 300grams of all purpose flour
  • 200g of granulated sugar (To get a darker colour of banana bread, Use 100g of granulated sugar and 100g of Brown sugar)
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 3 medium sizes of eggs
  • 2 tablespoons of milk
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla
  • 1/2 cup of vegetable oil

Procedure

  1. Peel the banana, put them in a bowl and mash with either a whisk, fork or wooden till you get a creamy texture.

  2. Using either a hand mixer or a hand whisk, Add vegetable oil and mix to combine.

  3. Add melted butter, eggs and milk into the banana mix then combine together.

  4. Add in the sugar and mix together (don't over mix)

  5. Add flour, baking powder and baking soda together in a separate bowl and mix together.

  6. Now pour in your dry ingredients into your banana bread batter( Wet ingredients) and mix to combine (do not over mix)

  7. Next place a parchment paper inside your pan or butter your pan then pour your banana bread batter into your pan and add any toppings of your choice (Oreos, granola, cashew nuts, coconut, carrot, oats, chocolate chips etc)

  8. Bake in a preheated oven at a temperature of 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 25 - 35 minutes till a tooth pick inserted in comes out clean.
